A river has separated Lolie from her family! Solve puzzles and collect leaves to help her build a boat and make her way back home.


Download and play!

Mac OS Windows

This game won first place out of ~20 teams at our EECS494 (UMich + EMU) showcase! It was developed in a group of 5 using Unity and C#. I worked mainly on the player movement physics code and state machine logic, along with some level design. Watch the trailor above!
