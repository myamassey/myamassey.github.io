Orbit

Take Earth on an adventure through the universe...but make sure to stay in the habitable zones!


Orbit was a rapid-prototype game that I created in 2 weeks using Unity and C#. Inspired by an exoplanets class I took in the fall, it was fun to incorporate some astronomy in this game.

You can play below by selecting a level. Hints: the white circles are the habitable zones; try to aim for those!
